Privacy Policy

Your privacy is critically important to us. The Stroop Game is designed with a "Privacy First" mindset, ensuring that your data stays exactly where it belongs: on your device.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • Location data or IP addresses.
  • Contact lists or calendar information.
  • Device identifiers or usage statistics.

2. Local Storage

All game-related data, including your coin balance, high scores, game statistics, and streak information, is stored locally on your device using an encrypted local database (Hive). This data never leaves your phone.

3. Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, tracking pixels, or advertising networks that could monitor your behavior or collect your data.

4. Data Deletion

Because all data is stored locally, you have full control over it. Uninstalling the app from your device will automatically delete all stored game data and preferences.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
